What this does
Upload your revised typology CSV from Phase 1 and a batch of new documents (5–10% of your corpus). Each document is coded against the fixed typology in an isolated session. The application flags any content that appears to represent a genuinely new concept not captured by the current typology. If new concepts are found, revise your typology and repeat. If the typology holds, proceed to Phase 3.
